About Us

Unicorn Global Scholars Ltd. delivers premium, values-based online education for learners worldwide. We align with international curricula, pairing academic support with mentorship, accountability, and moral guidance so every scholar grows in confidence, competence, and character.

Mission, Vision & Values

Our Promise to Scholars & Families

Mission

Deliver premium, values-based education that nurtures academic excellence, character development, and global readiness.

Vision

To be a globally trusted leader in transformative education, empowering scholars who positively impact their societies.

Core Values

Excellence, Integrity, Global Competence, Accountability, Innovation, and Student-Centeredness guide every class and interaction.

Our Services

Support for Scholars, Parents & Schools

Value-driven academic solutions tailored to global standards, wherever you learn or teach.

Academic Tutoring & Enrichment

Personalized one-on-one and small-group tutoring aligned with international curricula to build confidence, competence, and character.

  • British Curriculum: KS1–KS4, SATs, IGCSE/GCSE, A-Levels, BTEC, 11+, Checkpoints, Functional Skills.
  • American & Canadian Curricula: Grades 1–12 and High School Diploma programs.
  • Australian Curriculum: Years 1–12, NAPLAN, HSC, VCE, and IB programs.
  • Other international pathways with tailored learning plans worldwide.

Standardized Test Preparation

Strategic coaching for SAT, ACT, SSAT, GRE, IELTS, TOEFL, and GMAT focused on techniques, confidence, and score gains.

Learners practice with proven methods that unlock top opportunities for global advancement.

Teacher Recruitment & Staffing

Connecting schools with qualified, values-driven teachers for virtual and onsite roles through a rigorous, integrity-led selection process.

Every educator we place reflects UGS standards of excellence and professionalism.

Teacher Training & Professional Development

Workshops, masterclasses, and coaching on curriculum delivery, digital tools, and classroom management for continuous educator growth.

We cultivate innovation and confidence in every teacher we train.

Educational Support & Advisory Services

Our Teaching Model

We blend academic excellence with character development so every learner thrives—no matter where they are.

  • Identify and strengthen individual abilities while closing learning gaps.
  • Integrate moral values and emotional intelligence into daily learning.
  • Deliver dynamic, interactive lessons via Zoom and Google Meet.
  • Keep parents, teachers, and administrators connected through organized WhatsApp platforms.

Every learner is honored as a “Scholar,” encouraged to think critically, grow confidently, and learn with purpose.

Target Audience

We partner with learners, families, and institutions pursuing structured, values-based education.

  • British and international students across the UK, US, Canada, Africa, and beyond.
  • Parents seeking disciplined, purpose-driven learning for their children.
  • Schools and institutions needing qualified academic staff for onsite or virtual roles.
  • Aspiring and practicing teachers exploring global teaching opportunities.

Our tutors go beyond instruction—they inspire excellence and integrity in every class.
