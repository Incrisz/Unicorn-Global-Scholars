About-Us

Unicorn Global Scholars Ltd. is a premium online educational institution dedicated to nurturing academic excellence and moral integrity in learners across the world. The company provides structured, high-quality tutoring and mentorship aligned with international curricula and educational standards. Through a team of highly qualified educators, Unicorn Global Scholars delivers personalized academic support, continuous follow-up, and value-driven mentorship that foster confidence, competence, accountability, and holistic development. The institution remains steadfast in its commitment to maintaining consistent quality, moral integrity, and lifelong learning in every scholar under its care.

Our Teaching Model

We blend academic excellence with character development so every learner thrives—no matter where they are.

  • Identify and strengthen individual abilities while closing learning gaps.
  • Integrate moral values and emotional intelligence into daily learning.
  • Deliver dynamic, interactive lessons via Zoom and Google Meet.
  • Keep parents, teachers, and administrators connected through organized WhatsApp platforms.

Every learner is honored as a “Scholar,” encouraged to think critically, grow confidently, and learn with purpose.

Target Audience

We partner with learners, families, and institutions pursuing structured, values-based education.

  • British and international students across the UK, US, Canada, Africa, and beyond.
  • Parents seeking disciplined, purpose-driven learning for their children.
  • Schools and institutions needing qualified academic staff for onsite or virtual roles.
  • Aspiring and practicing teachers exploring global teaching opportunities.
