About-Us

Unicorn Global Scholars Ltd. is a premium online educational institution dedicated to nurturing academic excellence and moral integrity in learners across the world. The company provides structured, high-quality tutoring and mentorship aligned with international curricula and educational standards. Through a team of highly qualified educators, Unicorn Global Scholars delivers personalized academic support, continuous follow-up, and value-driven mentorship that foster confidence, competence, accountability, and holistic development. The institution remains steadfast in its commitment to maintaining consistent quality, moral integrity, and lifelong learning in every scholar under its care.

Mission Statement

Vision Statement

To be a globally trusted leader in transformative education—nurturing academic excellence, moral integrity, global competence, and raising scholars who positively impact their societies.

Core Values
